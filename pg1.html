What is Social Club?

Rockstar Games Social Club is the heart of our gaming community, offering players a unique platform to connect, compete, and collaborate. It serves as a hub where gamers can join crews, participate in events, and track their in-game progress across various Rockstar titles. With features like stat tracking, leaderboards, and exclusive bonuses, Social Club enhances the multiplayer experience and fosters camaraderie among players. Whether you're forming alliances in Grand Theft Auto Online, conquering the frontier in Red Dead Online, or engaging in adrenaline-fueled races in Midnight Club, Social Club provides the tools and infrastructure to elevate your gaming journey. Join the millions of players worldwide who call Social Club their digital home and unlock a wealth of content, rewards, and opportunities to showcase your skills.
